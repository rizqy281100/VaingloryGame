Vainglory Enters Cross-Platform Alpha

Welcome to the Vainglory PC and Mac alpha release. Vainglory is a cross-platform MOBA with the strategic depth and mechanical skill you’d expect from a PC title, but playable with your friends anywhere, on any device. In this video, we’re going to quickly cover some of the ways Vainglory’s design differs from the MOBAs you may already know.
